What is Brand Helper?

Brand Helper is an open creative-automation toolkit for solo founders and small teams. You describe a campaign (products, audience, message, aspect ratios) and it generates or reuses hero imagery, composites on-brand creatives in every ratio, and runs brand-compliance and legal checks — then lets you browse, edit, and export the results.

Requesting access

Brand Helper is invite-only. If you don’t have an invite yet, request one — an admin reviews every request.

  1. On the sign-in or landing page, click Request access.
  2. Enter your name, organization, and the email you’ll sign in with.
  3. An admin reviews your request and approves or declines it.
  4. If approved, you’ll receive a passwordless magic-link email — click it to sign in.
Go to sign-in

Signing in

Sign-in is passwordless. Enter your email on the sign-in page and we email you a one-time link — there’s no password to remember. Links are only sent to approved members, so if nothing arrives, you may not have access yet (see Requesting access). An admin can revoke or re-instate access at any time.

Using the pipeline

  1. New campaign — paste or build a brief (campaign name, products, audience, message, aspect ratios). Only a product name is required; sensible defaults fill the rest.
  2. Run — Brand Helper generates or reuses a hero per product, composites a creative in each aspect ratio, and runs compliance + legal checks.
  3. Review — open the run report for pass rates and flags, browse the creatives by product and ratio, refine one in the editor, and export.

Once signed in, the in-app Help page has a full walk-through, keyboard shortcuts, and example briefs you can run with one click.
